Census View
Currently this app dose not display the Census data in a engaging meaningful way. Soon I'm going start exploring the Census data and develop some nice interactive visualizations to help all the people understand the patterns of our little island.

Farmers Friend
The Central Statistics Office holds a lot CD data about and related to agricultural output. Agriculture as one of our biggest exports and the fabric of rural Ireland deserves some attention.

I'm working on a dash board to display all of the agricultural data and to bring data from other relevant sources. This will act as a one stop shop for farmers to get data for all things from Prices to weather.

Search
